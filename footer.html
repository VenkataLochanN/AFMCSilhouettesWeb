About

Silhouettes & AIIMBT is the annual cultural, sports and literary fest organized by the Armed Forces Medical College, Pune.
A crowd of over 5,000+ students from over 125 colleges all over India attended last year.
Silhouettes 2024 is all set to host a much larger crowd!
